Lasagna

This is a guide on how to make a delicious Lasagna for you and your family, step by step with ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion and Garlic Seasoning
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Oregano
  • Cream Cheese

Or if you prefer, you can add:

  • Spinach
  • Zucchini
  • Olives
  • Mushrooms
